My name is Po and living simple is my goal.

I used to be a pack rat I had so many things I thought I loved but I couldn't enjoy them because I had so much stuff. Things were horrible. I had a job I hated, a home that I didn't want to live in and I couldn't keep a relationship.

How I turned my life around

I was on the verge of a breakdown when I finally got rid of everything.I downsized to a smaller twig and go rid of 90% of my items. It wasn't easy to get rid of so many things that I had kept for so long, but I did it and I feel so much better for it.

Now I live the simple life

Now that I'm living a much more simpler life, things are so much better. I'm less stressed, I enjoy my work and life a lot more. I also have more free time to spend wih my fiance.

Because I turned my life around so well and I am so happy, I decided to start this site to share how other bugs can do it too and get to live the fullest life as well.
